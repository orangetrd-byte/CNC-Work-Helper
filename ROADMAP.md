# CNC Work Helper Roadmap

## Phase 1: Shop-Floor Utility Stability

- Keep setup notes, saved jobs, reference data, and movement calculators reliable.
- Maintain offline PWA behavior.
- Keep draft G-code and plotting clearly marked as helper aids.
- Maintain build information, version number, and MGP identity.

## Phase 2: Machinist Reference Depth

- Improve speeds and feeds, drill/tap, GD&T, and tooling references.
- Expand setup references without becoming a production scheduler.
- Add better validation and warnings for helper calculations.

## Phase 3: G-Code Assistance

- Improve G-code checking and explanation workflows.
- Add clearer toolpath preview behavior.
- Keep generated output draft-only and require operator verification.

## Phase 4: Shop Companion Polish

- Improve mobile speed and repeated-use ergonomics.
- Strengthen import/export and saved job workflows.
- Keep every feature useful at the machine in under 30 seconds.
